Hello!! I am "h2" tag. People use me to define the subtitle of the article. My default font size is 24px.

Hello!! I am "h3" tag. People use me to define the subtopics of the article. My default font size is 18.72px.

Hello!! I am "h5" tag. People use me to define the content at deep level. My default font size is 13.28px.
Hello!! I am "h6" tag. I'm very close to normal text. My default font size is 12px.

Hello!! I am "p" tag. I am a "paragraph". My default font size is 16px.

Hello!! I am "i" tag. People use me to style "italic" font.

Hello!! I am "b" tag. People use me to style "bold" font.

Hello!! I am "u" tag. People use me to write underline like this Sanjid Ara.

Hello!! I am "br" tag. People use me to "break" the line.

Hello!! I am "sub" tag. People use me to write any font down from the line like this "H2o".
